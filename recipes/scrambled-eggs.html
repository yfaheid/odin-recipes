Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Scrambled eggs is one the easiest dishes to make, which is why it is also one of the few that I can make.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Add some butter to hot pan
  2. Add eggs
  3. Scramble
  4. Finish with salt and pepper and serve
