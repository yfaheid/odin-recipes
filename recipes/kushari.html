Kushari Recipe

This is a recipe for Kushari, which is an Egyptian dish.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Macaroni
  • Lentils

Steps

  1. Cut the onion and tomatoes into chunks and put it into the blender along with garlic.
  2. Blend the ingredients
  3. Heat a pan with olive oil, and add the mix to the pan
  4. Add cooked rice and macaroni
  5. Mix and serve
