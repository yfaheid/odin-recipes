Grilled Cheese Recipe

Grilled Cheese is one the easiest dishes to make, which is why it is also one of the few that I can make.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Add some butter to hot pan
  2. Put cheese in bread
  3. Add sandwhich to pan
  4. Wait til crisp on one side then flip and do the same with the other side and serve
